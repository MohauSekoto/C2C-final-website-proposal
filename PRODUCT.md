# Product

## Register

product

## Users
South African local entrepreneurs (sellers) looking to sell nationally, and nationwide shoppers (buyers) seeking authentic local goods.

## Product Purpose
A C2C e-commerce platform that connects local SA businesses with buyers, providing built-in trust via escrow payments and automated shipping calculations. Success is measured by transaction volume and seller onboarding.

## Brand Personality
Clean, professional corporate marketplace injected with vibrant South African warmth (using Primary Blue and Secondary Gold). Highly dynamic, premium, and trustworthy.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS templates
- Simple or cheap MVP layouts
- Washed-out AI grays or bland neutral scaffolding

## Design Principles
- Trust through polish (escrow and shipping logic must feel incredibly robust).
- Vibrant but professional (use color purposefully to draw attention, not overwhelm).
- Seller empowerment (dashboards should feel like pro tools).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard baseline accessibility for now; specific targets (WCAG 2.1 AA contrast, reduced motion) will be formalized after the digital foundation is solid.

About KasiBuy

KasiBuy was founded with a single mission: to empower local sellers from townships (Kasis) and connect them with buyers nationwide in a secure, trust-less environment.

The Trust Problem

Many local artisans and traders struggle to reach markets outside their immediate vicinity. Buyers, on the other hand, are often hesitant to purchase from unknown sellers on social media due to the risk of scams.

Our Escrow Solution

We solve this by acting as a trusted intermediary. When a buyer makes a purchase, KasiBuy holds the funds securely in escrow. The seller is notified to ship the item, but they only receive the funds once the buyer receives and approves the delivery. If the item never arrives, the buyer is refunded. It's that simple.

Empowering Local Economies

By providing a professional storefront, secure payments via PayFast, and integrated shipping calculation, we lower the barrier to entry for local entrepreneurs, keeping money circulating within our communities.
